Simon Say Game

A Child Brain Booster Game

How to Play game?
Suppose In Level-1 A-Light blinks then Player must press A Button and Suppose In Level-2 light blinks in B-Button, Then Player must press A-Button before Pressing B-button and Similarly player must press all previous button in Sequence before pressing the Latest Blinked Button Otherwise Game Over
